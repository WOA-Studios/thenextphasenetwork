The day after

The day after your company dismissed you... the sun still rises.

The day after decades of faithful service were reduced to a meeting and a handshake... the sun still rises.

The day after your laptop was returned, your access was revoked, your projects reassigned, and your calendar disappeared... the sun still rises.

The day after you wondered if anyone would ever need what you know again... the sun still rises.

Not because yesterday did not matter. Not because what happened did not hurt. Not because you are expected to simply move on.
